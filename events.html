DevFest 2024

DevFest 2024 is a global tech event series organized by Google Developer Group. The event brings together developers, tech enthusiasts, and industry professionals to learn, collaborate, and explore the latest advancements in AI and other Google technologies. A key focus of this year's event is Responsible AI, which centers on the development of artificial intelligence technologies that promote productivity while ensuring ethical standards and social responsibility. DevFest occurs every year and offers opportunities for hands-on learning, discussions, and networking in areas such as AI, cloud computing, mobile, and web development.
